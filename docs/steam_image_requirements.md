# Steam Image Asset Requirements (UX/Publishing Report)

## 1. Capsules (Critical Store Assets)

**Types:** 
- Main Capsule (616×353) 
- Small Capsule (231×87) 
- Header Capsule (460×215) 
- Vertical Capsule (374×448) 
- Library Capsule (600×900) 
- Library Hero (3840×1240) 
- Library Logo (transparent PNG/SVG)

**Validation Criteria:** 
- Clear representation of the game 
- Readable text at small sizes 
- Avoid visual clutter 
- No misleading claims 
- No fake UI or non-gameplay representations 
- No unauthorized third-party logos

---

## 2. Screenshots

**Requirements:** 
- Minimum: 5 (Recommended: 8--10) 
- Resolution: ≥1280×720 (Ideal: 1920×1080+)

**Validation Criteria:** 
- Must be real gameplay 
- No fake renders or bullshots 
- UI allowed 
- No promotional overlays 
- Visual consistency with final product

---

## 3. Trailers / Videos

**Types:** 
- Gameplay trailer 
- Optional cinematic

**Validation Criteria:** 
- Gameplay must appear early 
- Avoid long intros 
- No misleading content

---

## 4. Promotional Artwork

**Includes:** 
- Key Art 
- Event/Sale artwork

**Criteria:** 
- High resolution (1080p minimum, ideally 4K base) 
- No watermarks 
- Content must match rating

---

## 5. Community & Library Assets

**Types:** 
- Library Capsule 
- Library Hero 
- Transparent Logo

**Criteria:** 
- Compatible with dark mode 
- Avoid plain white backgrounds 
- Logo separation for flexibility

---

## 6. Icons

**Types:** 
- App Icon (.ico, multiple sizes)

**Criteria:** 
- Legible at small sizes 
- Consistent branding 
- Avoid small text

---

## 7. General Requirements

**Technical:** 
- JPG/PNG formats 
- No compression artifacts 
- Exact aspect ratios

**Content:** 
- Must reflect actual gameplay 
- No misleading visuals 
- Must comply with rating

**Quality:** 
- Sharp images 
- Proper contrast 
- Clean composition

---

## 8. Common Rejection Reasons

-   Overloaded capsule designs
-   Non-gameplay screenshots
-   Stock or generic assets
-   Inconsistent branding
-   Illegible logos
-   Unverifiable claims

---

## 9. Recommended Asset Pipeline

Typical indie setup: 
- 6~8 capsule variants 
- 8~10 screenshots 
- 1~2 trailers 
- 1 key art source 
- Library asset set 
- Icon set

---

## Conclusion

Steam evaluates assets based on: 
1. Accuracy (no misleading content) 
2. Visual consistency

Beyond validation, assets directly impact store performance (CTR), making this a marketing-critical process.
